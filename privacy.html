Privacy Policy

Radioaktivitat Bot Detector collects and stores only the minimum data needed for moderation and analytics.

What we collect

  • Server and channel IDs for configuration (e.g., report channel)
  • User IDs and timestamps associated with flagged behavior
  • Optional code-drop logs (who clicked Reveal, when)

How we use it

  • To detect and report suspicious patterns related to airdrop abuse
  • To provide auditable, ephemeral code-drop reveals

Retention

Logs may be retained as long as needed for moderation. You may request deletion by contacting us.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share limited data when required by law or to investigate abuse.
